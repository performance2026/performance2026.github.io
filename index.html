IFIP WG 7.3 Performance 2026

3-5 November 2026, Ghent, Belgium

Welcome to Ghent

It is our pleasure to invite you to the IFIP WG 7.3 Performance 2026 conference, the 44th International Symposium on Computer Performance, Modelling, Measurements and Evaluation. The conference will be held at “De Krook” in Ghent, Belgium, on 3-5 November 2026.

This conference aims to bring together researchers interested in understanding and improving the performance of communication systems through state-of-the-art quantitative models and solution techniques.

The conference solicits long and short papers. The topics of interest and submission information are available in the call for papers.
