IFIP WG 7.3 Performance 2026

3-5 November 2026, Ghent, Belgium

Welcome to Ghent

It is our pleasure to invite you to the IFIP WG 7.3 Performance 2026 conference, the 44th International Symposium on Computer Performance, Modelling, Measurements and Evaluation. The conference will be held at “De Krook” in Ghent, Belgium, on 3-5 November 2026. This conference brings together researchers interested in understanding and improving the performance of communication systems through state-of-the-art quantitative models and solution techniques.

The conference solicits long and short papers. The topics of interest and submission information are available in the call for papers. We further welcome proposals for workshops that will take place on the conference’s workshop day, see the call for workshops for more information.

Important dates

  • Submission site opens: 15 March 2026
  • Abstract submission: 17 April 2026
  • Full paper submission: 24 April 2026
  • Notification to authors: 17 July 2026
  • Workshop proposals: April 1, 2026
